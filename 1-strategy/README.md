# Strategy

Market analysis, competitive intelligence, legislation, and violence exposure data by region.

## Contents

| Folder | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| `markets/` | Market sizing, population data, smartphone penetration, safety app adoption |
| `competition/` | Competitor analysis — Citizen, Neighbors (Ring), Life360, local apps |
| `legislation/` | Local law analysis per target market (data privacy, monopoly of force, civic apps) |
| `violence-exposure/` | Crime statistics, insecurity indices, underreported incident data by region |

## Key Questions This Folder Answers

- Where is the regulatory environment most permissive for a civilian alert app?
- Who are we competing with and what gaps exist?
- Which populations have the highest unmet need?
- What legal risks exist per market entry?
